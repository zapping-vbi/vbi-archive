Zapping Help

You can post patches, feature requests or bug reports at our Sourceforge tracker. Please log in or leave an e-mail address so we can contact you for further inquiries and progress notification.

If the problem is of a more complicated nature you can ask for support on our mailing list. You don't have to subscribe, but non-member messages may be delayed for spam filtering.

For minor questions and chit-chat you can try our IRC channel #zapping @ irc.freenode.net

Regarding private, legal and business matters you can contact the project administrator Michael H. Schimek < mschimek@users.sourceforge.net > directly. Ich spreche deutsch. You can find my PGP/GPG key on popular key servers and in the AUTHORS file included with the source code, e.g. type "gpg --import <AUTHORS".
